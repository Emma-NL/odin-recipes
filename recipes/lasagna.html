Italian Lasagna

Discription

Comforting, delicious and filling. This lasagna may take a bit of time, but it's so worth it. This recipe is originally for 12 servings, so its perfect to serve a larger group of people, or keep it as a leftover for another day!

Ingredients

Original recipe serves 12 people.

  • 1 pound italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 tablespoons fresh parsley
  • 1½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. In a large pot, cook the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until fully browned
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and some water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Cover this, and let this simmer for 1½ hours while stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring some salted water to a boil and cook the lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes. Rinse with cold water when done.
  5. In a bowl, mix some ricotta cheese with egg, the remaining parsley and salt.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. Spread about 1½ cups of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Place 6 noodles on top slightly overlapping. Add half of the ricotta mixture. Then add one third of the mozzarella slices. Pour another 1½ cups of meat sauce on top and finish with some Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat the process and add the remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese on top. Cover with foil.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then remove the foil, and bake for another 25 minutes.
  10. Let the lasagne rest for 15 minutes and serve. Enjoy!
